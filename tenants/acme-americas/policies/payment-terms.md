# Payment terms and scheduling policy

## 1. Scope

This policy governs the payment terms applied to approved vendor invoices across
Acme Inc. accounts-payable operations, and the scheduling of disbursements against
those terms.

## 2. Default terms

Unless a governing contract specifies otherwise, approved invoices are scheduled at
**Net 30** from the later of invoice date and goods-receipt date. Contract terms,
where a registered agreement exists for the vendor, override the default.

## 3. Early-payment discounts

Where a vendor offers an early-payment discount (for example, 2/10 Net 30), the
discount is captured only when the net present value of taking it exceeds Acme's
cost of capital for the period. The disbursement run evaluates this automatically.

## 4. Holds

An invoice under an open dispute, duplicate review, or goods-receipt mismatch is
held from scheduling until the blocking condition clears. Holds are visible on the
case and released only by the accountable reviewer.

## 5. Records

Every disbursement records the terms applied, the discount captured or forgone, and
any hold history, retained for the statutory audit period.
