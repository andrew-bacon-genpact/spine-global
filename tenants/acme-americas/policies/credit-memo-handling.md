# Credit memo handling policy

## 1. Scope

This policy governs the receipt, matching, and application of vendor credit memos
across Acme Inc. accounts-payable operations.

## 2. Matching

A credit memo is matched to the originating invoice or purchase order by vendor,
reference, and amount. A credit memo that cannot be matched to an underlying document
is routed for human review before application.

## 3. Application

A matched credit memo is applied against the next scheduled disbursement to the same
vendor. It is never refunded as a disbursement to the vendor except where a governing
contract requires it and a reviewer approves.

## 4. Aging

A credit memo unapplied after 90 days is escalated to the vendor-relations owner for
disposition, so that unused credits do not age off unnoticed.

## 5. Records

Every credit memo records its match, its application or escalation, and the reviewer
where one was involved, retained for the statutory audit period.
