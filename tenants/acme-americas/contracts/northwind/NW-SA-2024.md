# Northwind Supply — facilities service agreement (NW-SA-2024)

Summary of the governing service agreement between Acme Inc. and Northwind Supply,
mirrored for the Spine so recurring-billing checks can be grounded in contract terms.

## Parties

- **Buyer:** Acme Inc., Americas.
- **Vendor:** Northwind Supply, 1400 Commerce Park Drive, Columbus, OH 43219.

## Service and fee

Northwind provides recurring facilities services to Acme under a **fixed monthly
service fee of $1,200.00**. The fee is fixed for the term; it does not vary with
usage.

## Billing

- Billed **monthly** against blanket purchase order **PO-32011**.
- **Twelve invoices per year** are expected under this agreement — one per calendar
  month.
- Each monthly invoice is for the contracted amount of **$1,200.00**.
- Payment terms: **Net 30**.

Because the charge recurs at a fixed amount on a blanket PO, the twelve monthly
invoices are expected to be near-identical, differing only in invoice number and
document date. They are not duplicates.

## Term

- **Term:** FY2026.
- Renewable by agreement of the parties.
